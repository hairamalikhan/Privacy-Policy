1. Introduction

FlowFiler (“App,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is a taxation services application operated by Chughtai Developers in Pakistan. We help individuals and businesses file income tax returns, register NTN, and respond to tax notices with accuracy, confidentiality, and security.

Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information. By using FlowFiler, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please stop using the App.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect information with your explicit consent and only as needed for tax services. Before collecting, we show you a clear consent dialog explaining what is collected and why.

2.1 Personal Information (for tax filing services)

  • Full Name, CNIC, Date of Birth, Gender
  • Phone number, Email address, Mailing address
  • National Tax Number (NTN) when applicable

2.2 Financial & Tax Information

  • Salary, allowances, business income & expenses
  • Investments, assets, and liabilities relevant to tax filing
  • Tax-related documents (receipts, bank statements, etc.)
  • FBR credentials (only if you provide them for filing)

2.3 Supporting Documents

  • Tax notices and correspondence
  • Proof of income/identity for compliance purposes

2.4 Technical Information

  • Device model, OS, app version
  • Anonymous error/crash logs

2.5 What We Do NOT Collect

We never access:

  • Location/GPS
  • Contacts or call logs
  • Camera/microphone data
  • Social media accounts
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • Tax Filing & NTN Registration: Preparing and submitting returns to FBR
  • Document Handling: Processing documents securely and confidentially
  • Compliance: Meeting legal requirements under Pakistan tax laws
  • Customer Support: Communicating service progress and completion
  • Security: Protecting against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage (anonymized) to improve services

We do not sell, trade, or use your data for marketing/advertising.

4. Sharing of Information

We share your data only when strictly necessary:

  • FBR & Government Authorities: For compliance with tax laws
  • Authorized Service Providers: e.g., secure cloud storage, IT maintenance, and certified tax consultants
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or court order

We never share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated third parties.

5. Data Security

We apply strong technical and organizational security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption (AES-256 + RSA hybrid & HTTPS)
  • No local storage of sensitive data on your phone
  • Role-based staff access with confidentiality agreements
  • Regular audits, monitoring, and backups

Despite strict measures, no system is 100% risk-free. If you suspect misuse, contact us immediately.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Tax records: 7 years (as required under Pakistan law)
  • Support communications: 3 years
  • Technical logs: 90 days

You can request deletion anytime via Settings > Delete My Data or email. Data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours, except where law requires retention.

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Consent Management: Grant or withdraw consent anytime
  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Fix errors in your records
  • Deletion: Request complete data removal

To exercise these rights, contact us at flowfiler22@gmail.com. We respond within 7 business days.

FlowFiler is not designed for children under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If such data is found, it will be deleted immediately.
